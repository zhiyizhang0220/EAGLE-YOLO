# Field trial assets

This repository includes field closed-loop intra-row weeding trial assets:

- `Field Trial Data.xlsx`: spreadsheet data from field trials.
- `Field Trial Video.mp4`: video record from field trials.

The manuscript reports that EAGLE-YOLO reduced crop damage from 18.47% to 7.59%, increased weeding success from 77.78% to 89.99%, and reduced invalid actuation from 37.64% to 8.41% in closed-loop field trials.

The video and spreadsheet are tracked with Git LFS because they are binary research assets.
